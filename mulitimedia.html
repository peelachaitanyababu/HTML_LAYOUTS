Hello


Hello! is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language romantic action film written and directed by Vikram K Kumar, and produced by Nagarjuna Akkineni under Annapurna Studios banner. The film stars Akhil Akkineni and Kalyani Priyadarshan. The music is composed by Anup Rubens. It was released on 22 December 2017.

Music Director: Anup Rubens
